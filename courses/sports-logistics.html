Course · MGT 3173

Sports Logistics

Undergraduate Operations
Course Description

Principles in sports logistics, with instruction emphasizing intercollegiate athletics and practical application. Provides an understanding of all phases of intercollegiate sports logistics, with opportunities for hands-on learning and guest speakers.

Course Learning Objectives / Outcomes
  • Demonstrate an understanding of the principles and practices of planning, financing, marketing, implementing, evaluating, and coordinating logistical and facility considerations for sport events and venues.
  • Identify and discuss current trends and emerging issues facing sport event and venue managers.
  • Demonstrate an understanding of the differences between various types of venues and the logistical considerations a venue and event manager must weigh to operate and lead successfully.
  • Explain the concept of risk management and analyze the significance of legal considerations in sport event and venue operations.
  • Critically evaluate a sport event in consideration of preparation, organization, implementation, and outcomes.
