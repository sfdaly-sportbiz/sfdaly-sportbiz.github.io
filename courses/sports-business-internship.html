Course · MGT 3700

Sports Business Internship

Undergraduate Experiential
Course Description

The internship experience provides students with the opportunity to work in positions related to their field of study and to gain valuable professional experience while enhancing their academic career. An internship normally covers one academic semester and may be either paid or unpaid.

Professional Development Outcomes
  • Apply classroom knowledge to real tasks and challenges within a sport organization.
  • Practice workplace professionalism, including communication, time management, and collaboration in a professional setting.
  • Build and begin to leverage a professional network within the sport industry.
  • Reflect critically on personal strengths, growth areas, and career direction based on hands-on experience.
  • Translate the internship experience into a polished resume, portfolio, and professional narrative for future opportunities.

Enrolled students: course materials, schedule, and assignments are on Canvas.
