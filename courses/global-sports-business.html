Course · MGT 5322

Global Sports Business

Graduate (MBA) Global & Strategy
Course Description

This dynamic course provides a comprehensive exploration of the global landscape of sports business, examining the intricacies of international markets, cross-cultural management, and the unique challenges and opportunities within the sports industry. Participants delve into the strategic, financial, and ethical dimensions of international sports business, gaining insights to navigate the complexities of a rapidly evolving global sports economy.

Learning Objectives
  • Develop a strategic mindset with the ability to analyze and formulate international sports business strategies, understanding the impact of cultural, economic, and political factors on market entry, expansion, and sustainable growth.
  • Navigate and succeed in cross-cultural communication, negotiation, and management specific to the sports business context, including building effective international teams and managing diverse stakeholders.
  • Understand the structures and mechanisms of international sport governance, including the role of international organizations, legal frameworks, and ethical considerations.
  • Explore the intersection of technology and innovation in the global sports industry, and analyze the implications of emerging technologies and data analytics on athlete performance, fan engagement, and business operations.
