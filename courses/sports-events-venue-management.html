Course · MGT 5312

Sports Events and Venue Management

Graduate (MBA) Venues & Events
Course Description

This course covers various topics related to the development and management of sports venues, including stadiums, arenas, ballparks, and training facilities. Topics include design considerations, internal and external funding, revenue generation and management, politics, and emerging technologies.

Course Learning Outcomes
  • Develop a comprehensive understanding of key design considerations in the development and renovation of sports venues, and evaluate how design choices impact fan experience, safety, and operational efficiency.
  • Analyze and implement diverse financial strategies — public-private partnerships, sponsorships, and government grants — and demonstrate proficiency in creating and managing revenue streams through financial planning and budgeting.
  • Explore the impact of political factors on sports venue development and management, and formulate strategies to navigate approvals, permits, and community support for venue projects.
  • Examine and apply the latest technological advancements in sports venue management, integrating innovations such as augmented reality, data analytics, and sustainable practices into venue operations.
