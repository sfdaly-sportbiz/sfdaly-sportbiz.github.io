Course · MGT 3193

Sports Marketing & Management

Undergraduate Marketing
Course Description

An introduction to sports marketing management designed to expose students to the concepts of collegiate sports marketing while giving real-world, behind-the-scenes exposure to managing a major college athletics department through the marketing side of the industry.

Course Objectives
  • Explain the fundamental concepts of sports marketing, including the sports marketing mix, consumer behavior, and the unique characteristics of sports products and services.
  • Identify and describe the key elements of sports sponsorships, endorsements, and promotional strategies used across sports industry segments.
  • Apply basic marketing principles to analyze sports consumer segments and develop appropriate targeting strategies.
  • Demonstrate understanding of digital marketing and social media strategies in sports, including fan engagement and content creation.
  • Evaluate a basic sports marketing plan that incorporates fundamental marketing concepts, ethical considerations, and strategic planning elements.
