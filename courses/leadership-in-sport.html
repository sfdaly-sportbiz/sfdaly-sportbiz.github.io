Course · MGT 3333

Leadership in Sport

Undergraduate Leadership
Course Description

This course explores the principles of leadership as they apply to the sport industry. Students learn about various leadership styles and theories and develop their own leadership skills through case studies, simulations, and real-world examples. The course also examines the unique challenges and opportunities that leaders in the sport industry face, covering topics such as managing conflict, building and leading teams, and navigating organizational politics.

Course Objectives
  • Define leadership and identify key leadership theories and styles.
  • Analyze and evaluate the effectiveness of different leadership approaches in the sport industry.
  • Analyze various employee and organizational factors that impact leadership.
  • Understand the unique challenges and opportunities that leaders in the sport industry face and navigate them effectively.
  • Apply leadership principles to real-world scenarios in the sport industry.
