Recent Projects

NIL Valuation Model

Nov '24 – Jul '25

University of Oklahoma Athletics

Challenge: With NIL revenue-sharing arriving in college athletics, OU Athletics needed a defensible way to value individual football players.

What I did: Researched existing valuation models and applicable variables, then worked directly with coaches and administrators to weight them for OU's program.

Outcome: Delivered a statistical valuation model now in active use for football player valuation.

Arena Renovation Feasibility Study

Winter 2023

University of Oklahoma Athletics

Challenge: Determine whether and how to bring the Lloyd Noble Center up to SEC-level standard.

What I did: Led a feasibility study with undergraduate students, including financial analysis of seat upgrades, investment cost, and projected ROI.

Outcome: Delivered renovation recommendations and financial projections directly to athletics administration.

Economic Impact Analysis, PGA Korn Ferry Tour

June 2023

Norman Local Organizing Committee

Challenge: The LOC needed to decide whether a five-year contract with the PGA at OU's Jimmy Austin golf course was worth the commitment.

What I did: Conducted an economic impact analysis of the event's effect on Norman, OK.

Outcome: Analysis directly informed the LOC's decision on the five-year annual event agreement.
